About James

James is a multi-faceted artist with interests and skills that span a wide array of mediums and forms. Though he works primarily within the realms animation, paper arts, and illustration, he never shies away from experiencing new forms of expression. He is also familiar with the projection and mural arts.

Residing in Santa Cruz CA, this 26 yr old native of Philadelphia continues to explore the forms that connect us all in order to create work that reflects the patterns of the universe within and around us.
